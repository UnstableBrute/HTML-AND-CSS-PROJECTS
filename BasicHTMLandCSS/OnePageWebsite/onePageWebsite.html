Scrambled Eggs

There are a handful of ways to cook scrambled eggs, although my personal favorite is to crack the eggs all into a bowl(make sure to not get any shells in there!), mix them up, then put them into a hot pan with oil/butter. After that make sure to keep mixing them, and once they start to solidify, add whatever seasonings that you want. Once all the eggs are solid it is ready to eat whichever way you want, just make sure to turn off the stovetop and enjoy!
